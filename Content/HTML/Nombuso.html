Biography and Background

Nombuso Mdletshe was born in Durban, and she went to school in Vryheid. She studies BSc Life and Earth Science and majors in Genetics.

Hobbies

Nombuso finds joy in immersive reading, where every book becomes an adventure. Beyond the pages, she expresses her creativity through crocheting, weaving warmth and artistry into every stitch. The meditative rhythm of her craft provides a sense of relaxation and fulfillment.
